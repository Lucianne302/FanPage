Background

Amy Lee is better known as the lead vocalist of the rock band Evanescence. Since Amy plays the classical piano, she likes to mix "multiple dramas, from the dramatic to the rock to the classical". She has a solo career and has worked with other great artists like David Hodges, Lindsey Stirling and groups like Korn amd Seether. She has received the Songwriter Icon Award from the National Music Publishers Association, she won Best Vocalist at the Revolver Golden Gods Awards, Rock Goddess of the Year at the Loudwire Music Awards, and Best Film Score by the Moondance International Film Festival.

As a philantropist, Amy Lee became a chairperson for the international epilepsy awareness foundation, Out of the Shadows, in memory of a sibiling that was affected by epilepsy. Currently, Amy Lee is focused on creating childrens books and music.

The reasons I like Amy Lee are:
